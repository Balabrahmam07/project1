Training and Placement Cell



WELCOME TO ACE PLACEMENT CELL


Every student has aspirations and looks for the best support to realize the dream of landing in a reputed organization, through
campus drives visiting the campus. Training and Placement Cell plays a critical role in identifying job opportunities for the students
who aspire to jumpstart their professional life after completion of their engineering. T&P cell recognizes that training is important for
engineering students to enhance their employability skills and achieve good placement in various industries. T&P Cell operates round
the year in understanding the skills requirements of industry and its expectations, mapping the requirements with the interest domains
of students, plan, successfully implement training sessions, and integrate the training modules into the regular academics where
necessary and facilitate these students by keeping in touch with reputed firms and industrial establishments.

While competition is on a high, opportunities are aplenty for the right candidate. Defining this RIGHT is the primary objective of the
T&P Cell. Training of students and equipping them with life skills is the foremost responsibility of the T&P Cell. The Cell @ACE strives
to impart technical expertise while nurturing the student for holistic personality.
