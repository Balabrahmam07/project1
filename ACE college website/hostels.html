Girls Hostel


Our Girls Hostel provides a safe and supportive environment for you to thrive during your engineering studies. Here's what you can expect:

Modern and Secure: Well-furnished rooms with comfortable beds, dedicated study areas, and ample storage space. 24/7 security ensures your peace of mind.

Focus on Wellbeing: Spacious common areas for relaxation and socializing. Dedicated wardens to address any concerns and provide guidance.

Healthy and Convenient: Nutritious and hygienic meals served in a spacious cafeteria. Laundry facilities to keep you organized.

Stay Connected: High-speed Wi-Fi throughout the hostel to stay connected with family and friends.

Boys Hostel

The Boys Hostel at ACE offers a vibrant and stimulating environment to focus on your studies and build lifelong friendships.
Designed for Success: Comfortable rooms with dedicated study areas and high-speed Wi-Fi to support your academic pursuits.
Comradery and Support: Spacious common areas for recreation, including game rooms and TV lounges. Dedicated wardens to ensure a smooth and enjoyable stay.
Looking After Yourself: Laundry facilities available for your convenience. Hygienic and nutritious meals served in a spacious cafeteria.
Stay Active: Access to on-campus sports facilities to stay fit and healthy.
